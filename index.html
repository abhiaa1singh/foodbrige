About Us

Bridging the Gap
Between Surplus & Need

Every year, millions of tons of food go to waste while millions of people go hungry. FoodBridge connects restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals with surplus food to local shelters and food banks.

FAQ

Common Questions

What types of food can I donate?

We accept most non-perishable items, fresh produce, baked goods, and prepared meals from commercial kitchens. All food must be within its expiration date and properly stored.

How quickly can you pick up donations?

For perishable items, we can often arrange same-day pickup. For non-perishables, scheduling is flexible. Emergency pickups are available for large quantities.

Do you provide tax receipts?

Yes! All donations receive a detailed receipt for tax purposes. Corporate partners receive additional documentation for CSR reporting and compliance.

How do you ensure food safety?

Our volunteers are trained in food safety protocols. We maintain cold chain for perishables, use sanitized containers, and follow all local health department guidelines.
